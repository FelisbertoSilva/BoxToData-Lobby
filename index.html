Hardwood Hoops

I built this platform to bring a sense of realism to a ProBoards simulation league that the native forum software couldn't. It moves the league away from messy threads and limited stats display into a dedicated space where members can track live transactions, analyze box scores, and dive into expanded player/team stats. By centralizing years of historical data into one UI, the goal was to make the front-office experience feel less like a forum game and more like the actual NBA.

Last updated league information: December 31, 2025 (with exception of transactions where one was used as a test sample)
